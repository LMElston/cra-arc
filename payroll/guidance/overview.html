Overview

An individual's employment status helps determine, the:

  • Individual's rights
  • Employer's responsibilities

For income tax, an individual may have a different employment status.

Types of employment status

The main types of employment status are:

  • Worker
  • Employee
  • Self-employed and contractor
  • Director
  • Office holder

Advice about employment status, employee rights or employer responsibilities

You may contact the Employment and Social Development Canada for general information about the employee rights or employer responsibilities.

Courts and tribunals can make final decisions on employment status.
