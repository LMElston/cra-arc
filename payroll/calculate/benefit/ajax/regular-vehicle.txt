<p>The CRA considers the <strong>regular place of employment </strong>any location where your employee <strong>regularly</strong>:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Reports for work </li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Performs the duties of employment  </li>
</ul>
<p>"Regularly" means the degree of frequency or repetition in the employee's reporting to a work location in a period (weekly, monthly or yearly) . <strong>This does not need to be your establishment</strong>.</p>
<p>Generally, any travel by the employee between work and regular place of employment is considered personal driving.</p>
<details><summary>Examples</summary>
<p>For example:</p>
<ul>
<li>Office where your employee reports daily </li>
<li>Several store locations that a manager visits monthly </li>
<li>Client's premises when your employee reports daily for a 6 month project </li>
<li>Client's premises when your employee attends biweekly meetings </li>
</ul>
</details>
