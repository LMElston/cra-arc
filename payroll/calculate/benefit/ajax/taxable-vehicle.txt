<h4>Non-taxable situation</h4>
<p>If you <a href="#availability" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">make a vehicle available&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> to your employee, the benefit is <strong>not </strong>taxable if all of the following apply:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and">
<li class="cnjnctn-col"> There is <strong><a href="#personal" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">no personal driving&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></strong> </li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col"> Use of the vehicle was entirely in the course of their employment duties </li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col"> The vehicle was returned to the premises of the employer at the end of the work day</li>
</ul>

<h4>Taxable situation</h4>
<p>If you <a href="#availability" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">make a vehicle available &nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> to your employee and they use the vehicle for <strong><a href="#personal" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">both business and personal driving&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></strong>, the personal use is taxable.</p>
