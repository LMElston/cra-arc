Automatic Exchange of Information (AEOI) – CRS and FATCA

The information provided on this page is intended to help Canadian Financial Institutions (FIs) and their account holders understand their responsibilities relating to Canada's participation in the automatic exchange of financial account information with foreign jurisdictions. The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is responsible for data collection of financial account information and exchanges with foreign jurisdictions as part of an ongoing effort to combat tax evasion, encourage voluntary compliance and ensure a fair tax system for all. Canada participates in two automatic exchanges of financial account information:

  • Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA)
  • Common Reporting Standard (CRS)
